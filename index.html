NASA Community College Aerospace Scholars (NCAS)

When I transferred to Collin College, I learned about the NCAS program, which is designed for community college students. I wanted to take advantage of this program since I took part in HAS, it felt like a continuation and I wanted to take part in this program to continue to grow my knowledge and continue my journey in the Space Industry.

Here is the description of what I did with the NCAS Program during all of the 3 missions it has:
  • Mission 1: Discover occurs July 26 - August 30, 2023 online. The course includes 5 weekly modules containing readings, videos, short assignments, a NASA subject matter expert (SME) speaker series, and a Final Infographic Assignment. The NCAS team assists with course delivery, office hours, and evaluation of submitted student work.
  • Mission 2: Explore has 2 educational simulation experiences. I was assigned the career simulation which is 1 week long from October 30th - November 3rd. The career simulation situates students to take on mock roles as NASA employees to gain exposure to workplace structure and culture. This simulation highlights the personal responsibilities and team dynamics needed to accomplish a huge goal such as a lunar surface exploration mission.
  • Mission 3: Innovate is a Capstone Project that has 2 parts, 2 weeks of virtual from January 11-26, 2024 and 1 week onsite at NASA Langley Research Center from January 29 - February 2, 2024. I participated in a capstone problem based on current challenges faced by NASA mission directorates. I developed possible solutions to the capstone problem and report them to a panel of NASA guests.
